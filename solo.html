Top three activities to do at Chennai

Perfect for a leisurely stroll

Marina beach is a popular spot for morning walks, enjoying the sunrise, and tasting local street food

A serene and historic atmosphere for visitors

The Santhome church is renowned for its stunning neo-Gothic architecture, featuring a tall spire, stained glass windows, and intricate interior designs

Dravidian architecture and vibrant cultural festivals

Kapaleeshwara Temple is a bustling cultural and religious hub, especially during festivals, offering a deep insight into Chennai's spiritual heritage.
